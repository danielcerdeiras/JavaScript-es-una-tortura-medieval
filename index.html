A N T I S E N

"The game we didn't really feel like doing but had to anyways"
Description
You think this web page is dope? Probably not, but just wait until you see the incredible, revolutionary, not-made-in-for-hours-before-the-deadline videogame we have in store for you!


A N T I S E N is a collage project that we had (against our will) to program in JavaScript. The result is a top-down, turn-based game in which the enemies are polite enough to wait for you to move in order to attack. Your objective is to get to the end of each level avoiding being squashed, shot or humilliated by your enemies. To do so, you can choose one power to help you get though the dungeons, but remember, we are not reponsable for potential bugs in the code.
Have fun!
